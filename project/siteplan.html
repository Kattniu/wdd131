Site Plan for The Influence of AI in the World

1. SITE NAME

Proposed Name: The Influence of AI in the World

Reason for Selection: This name reflects the site's focus on informing the public about the impact of artificial intelligence on society and the benefits it offers.

Optional Domain Availability: theinfluenceofai.com

2. SITE PURPOSE

The purpose of the site is to educate visitors about artificial intelligence, covering:

  • What is AI? A brief introduction to the concept and history of artificial intelligence.
  • Benefits of AI: How AI is transforming various sectors and enhancing efficiency and innovation.
  • Challenges and Opportunities: Discussing ethical concerns, job displacement, and future developments in AI technology.

3. SCENARIOS

Scenario 1: A high school student researching AI for a school project wants to understand the basic concepts and applications of AI.

Scenario 2: A business professional looking for information on how to implement AI in their industry to enhance productivity and decision-making.

Scenario 3: A concerned citizen interested in learning about the ethical implications of AI and its impact on job security.

Scenario 4: An entrepreneur seeking opportunities to leverage AI for business growth and innovation.

4. COLOR SCHEMA

Primary Color: #003366 (Dark Blue) - Used for headings and navigation elements, conveying professionalism and trust.

Secondary Color: #CCCCCC (Light Gray) - Used for backgrounds and sections, providing a clean and modern look.

Accent Color: #FFFFFF (White) - Used for text and buttons to enhance readability and contrast.

Text Color: #000000 (Black) - For body text to ensure high contrast and legibility.

5. TYPOGRAPHY

Font 1: Roboto - Montserrat - providing a modern and clean look.

Font 2: Open Sans - Used for body text, offering excellent readability.

Font 3: Arial - Used for buttons and forms, ensuring compatibility across devices.

Font 4: Afacad Flux - Used for headings.
